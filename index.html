ABOUT US

Maatii Boorenaa Charity Association (MBCA) is a non-governmental, non-profit, community-based association founded in September 2022 for the purpose of serving the community in the improvement of the social wellbeing by addressing critical problems focusing on health, nutritional and educational services. The association is established to serve the interests of communities in Daka Delu Harangema kebele, Negelle Arsi wereda, west Arsi Zone, Oromia Regional State. Creating a Brighter Future

Geda Academy

Geda Academy is a pre-elementary school developed by Maatii Booranaa Charity Association with the goal of providing free education, food, and other related things for underprivileged kids. The school is in Langanoo, Ethiopia, and it currently serves over 100 children. Geda Academy is an asset to the community of Langanoo. The school is providing a quality education to underprivileged children who would not otherwise could learn. Geda Academy is also helping to improve the lives of the children's families. The school is providing food and other essential services to the families, and it is helping to create a brighter future for the children.

MISSION

The overall aim of Maatii Booranaa Charity Association is to serve the community in social wellbeing by providing aid in health, food items and schooling materials for those highly affected part of community. We strive to empower individuals and families to lead healthier and more productive lives. We are committed to building a strong and sustainable future for our community and to making a positive impact on society.

community-based Plan

Goals

  • Provide free education to underprivileged children in Langano, Ethiopia.
  • Provide free healthcare for the community.
  • Provide access to clean water.
  • To create sustainable income streams to be maintained by the community, and develop their economic prowess.
  • Provide food and other essential services to the children's families.
  • Help to improve the lives of the children and their families.
  • Break the cycle of poverty in the community.
  • Make a difference in the lives of the children and in the community.
  • Be an asset to the community of Langano.
  • Be a success story.
